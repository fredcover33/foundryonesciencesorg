BUSINESS ASSOCIATE AGREEMENT

This Business Associate Agreement ("BAA") is entered into between Foundry One Sciences LLC, a Delaware Limited Liability Company ("Business Associate"), and the undersigned licensed healthcare provider ("Covered Entity / Physician").

1. Definitions

Protected Health Information (PHI) means any individually identifiable health information as defined under 45 C.F.R. § 160.103. De-identified Information means health information processed to remove all 18 HIPAA Safe Harbor identifiers per 45 C.F.R. § 164.514(b).

2. Obligations of Business Associate

Foundry One Sciences LLC agrees to: (a) not use or disclose PHI other than as permitted; (b) use appropriate safeguards; (c) report any breach within 72 hours; (d) ensure subcontractors agree to equivalent restrictions; (e) make records available to HHS; (f) return or destroy PHI upon termination.

3. Permitted Uses and Disclosures

Business Associate may use PHI solely to perform research intelligence services. PHI will be de-identified prior to MRS Engine processing. No PHI will be used for unrelated purposes.

4. Security Obligations

AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit, MFA-enforced portal access, immutable audit logging, and automatic session timeout after 15 minutes.

5. Term and Termination

Effective upon electronic acceptance. Either party may terminate upon 30 days notice. PHI destroyed within 30 days of termination with written certification.

6. Governing Law

Governed by HIPAA/HITECH and the laws of the State of Delaware.

Access Not Granted

You must accept the Business Associate Agreement to access the portal.
