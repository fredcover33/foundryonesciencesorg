BUSINESS ASSOCIATE AGREEMENT

This Business Associate Agreement ("BAA") is entered into between Foundry One Sciences LLC, a Delaware Limited Liability Company ("Business Associate"), and the undersigned licensed healthcare provider ("Covered Entity / Physician"), collectively referred to as the "Parties."

1. Definitions

Protected Health Information (PHI) means any individually identifiable health information transmitted or maintained in any form or medium, as defined under 45 C.F.R. § 160.103. De-identified Information means health information that has been processed to remove all 18 HIPAA Safe Harbor identifiers per 45 C.F.R. § 164.514(b).

2. Obligations of Business Associate

Foundry One Sciences LLC agrees to: (a) not use or disclose PHI other than as permitted by this BAA or required by law; (b) use appropriate safeguards to prevent unauthorized use or disclosure; (c) report any unauthorized use or disclosure to the Covered Entity within 72 hours; (d) ensure that any subcontractors agree to equivalent restrictions; (e) make internal practices and records available to HHS as required; (f) return or destroy PHI upon termination of this agreement.

3. Permitted Uses and Disclosures

Business Associate may use and disclose PHI solely to perform research intelligence services on behalf of the Covered Entity. PHI will be de-identified prior to processing by the MRS Engine. De-identified research outputs are not PHI and are not subject to HIPAA restrictions. No PHI will be used for independent purposes unrelated to the services described herein.

4. Security Obligations

Business Associate shall implement administrative, physical, and technical safeguards that reasonably protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of PHI. This includes AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit, MFA-enforced portal access, immutable audit logging, and automatic session timeout after 15 minutes of inactivity.

5. Term and Termination

This BAA is effective upon electronic acceptance and remains in force for the duration of the service relationship. Either party may terminate upon 30 days written notice. Upon termination, Business Associate shall destroy all PHI within 30 days and provide written certification of destruction.

6. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by HIPAA/HITECH and applicable federal regulations. Any disputes shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.

Access Not Granted

You must accept the Business Associate Agreement to access the Foundry One Sciences Physician Portal. This requirement is mandated by federal law under HIPAA.
