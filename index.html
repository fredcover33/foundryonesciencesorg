🔒 Encryption & Security

All PHI transmitted through this portal is encrypted using AES-256 end-to-end encryption. Sessions are authenticated per access and automatically terminated after inactivity.

  • ✓ AES-256 encryption on all PHI fields
  • ✓ TLS 1.3 in-transit encryption
  • ✓ Session timeout after 15 minutes inactivity
  • ✓ Access logs maintained per 45 CFR §164.312
📄 Business Associate Agreement

Foundry One Sciences LLC maintains an active Business Associate Agreement (BAA) with all participating physician practices. The BAA governs the use, disclosure, and safeguarding of PHI submitted through the MRS Engine portal.

  • ✓ BAA required before portal access granted
  • ✓ PHI used exclusively for clinical decision support
  • ✓ No PHI shared with third parties without authorization
  • ✓ Breach notification protocol active per 45 CFR §164.400
⚕️ SaMD Output Framing

The MRS Engine is framed as a Software as a Medical Device (SaMD) clinical decision support tool. All outputs are advisory in nature and do not constitute a diagnosis, prescription, or treatment recommendation.

  • ✓ All outputs labeled as clinical decision support
  • ✓ Physician judgment supersedes all MRS outputs
  • ✓ FDA SaMD framing compliance maintained
  • ✓ Audit trail on all queries and outputs
📋 Minimum Necessary Standard

PHI intake is limited to the minimum necessary information required to generate a clinically relevant MRS Engine query. Physicians are instructed not to submit PHI beyond what is required for the specific clinical question.

  • ✓ Intake fields limited to clinically necessary data
  • ✓ De-identification option available for research queries
  • ✓ Retention policy: 6 years per HIPAA standard
