BUSINESS ASSOCIATE AGREEMENT

This Business Associate Agreement ("BAA") is entered into between Foundry One Sciences LLC, a Delaware Limited Liability Company ("Business Associate"), and the undersigned licensed healthcare provider ("Physician"), collectively referred to as the "Parties."

1. Purpose

1.1 This BAA governs the terms under which the Physician may access the Foundry One Sciences Physician Portal and the Medical Research Solitaire (MRS) Engine clinical decision support platform ("Platform").

1.2 The Parties acknowledge that in the course of using the Platform, the Physician may submit Protected Health Information ("PHI") as defined under HIPAA, as amended by HITECH.

2. Permitted Uses and Disclosures

2.1 Foundry One Sciences LLC is permitted to use and disclose PHI only for the purpose of providing clinical decision support services through the MRS Engine Platform as directed by the Physician.

2.2 Foundry One Sciences LLC shall not use or disclose PHI in any manner that would violate HIPAA/HITECH requirements.

2.3 PHI submitted through the Platform shall not be sold, shared with third parties, or used for any commercial purpose without explicit written authorization.

3. Safeguards

3.1 Foundry One Sciences LLC agrees to implement appropriate administrative, physical, and technical safeguards per 45 CFR §164.312.

3.2 All PHI is encrypted using AES-256 end-to-end encryption and TLS 1.3 in-transit encryption.

3.3 Access logs are maintained for all PHI interactions per HIPAA audit control requirements.

4. Breach Notification

4.1 Foundry One Sciences LLC shall notify the Physician of any breach of unsecured PHI within sixty (60) calendar days of discovery per 45 CFR §164.400–414.

4.2 Notification shall include the nature of PHI involved, unauthorized persons involved, and mitigation steps taken.

5. Minimum Necessary Standard

5.1 The Physician agrees to submit only the minimum necessary PHI required to generate a clinically relevant MRS Engine query.

5.2 De-identification of patient data is available and encouraged for research-oriented queries.

6. SaMD Clinical Decision Support Acknowledgment

6.1 The Physician acknowledges that the MRS Engine is a Software as a Medical Device (SaMD) clinical decision support tool. All outputs are advisory in nature.

6.2 MRS Engine outputs do not constitute a diagnosis, prescription, or treatment recommendation. Physician clinical judgment supersedes all Platform outputs at all times.

6.3 Foundry One Sciences LLC shall not be held liable for clinical decisions made on the basis of Platform outputs.

7. Term and Termination

7.1 This BAA is effective upon electronic acceptance and shall remain in effect for the duration of the Physician's platform access.

7.2 Either Party may terminate upon thirty (30) days written notice. Upon termination, PHI shall be returned or destroyed.

7.3 PHI retention complies with the HIPAA standard of six (6) years.

8. Governing Law

8.1 This BAA shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware and applicable federal law including HIPAA and HITECH.

9. Electronic Acceptance

9.1 By completing the fields below and checking the acknowledgment boxes, the Physician agrees to be legally bound by the terms of this BAA. Electronic acceptance constitutes a valid binding signature under the E-SIGN Act.
